Our Story

703 million people lack basic access to clean and safe drinking water. We've been on a mission to end the water crisis since 2006, and with the help of generous supporters like you, we're getting closer every day. Charity: Water offers a donation model that guarantees 100% of all the funds donated go directly to clean water projects, providing full transparency and real-time updates. To support the initiative, Charity: Water provides a fast and trustworthy way to donate, providing a way for even small donations to have big impacts.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
